## Details

The Gull at the Minden Wild Water Preserve is Ontario's slalom course: a short, dam-controlled Class III–IV boulder-garden channel that hosted the 2015 Pan Am Games. When the release is on, it's the most reliable technical whitewater in the province; when it's off, it's a rock garden.

## Levels

Course flow is set by MNR dam releases from Horseshoe Lake upstream, not by rainfall. The federal gauge at Norland sits well downstream (below Gull Lake) and lags the course by many hours — use it as a general indicator of how much water is moving through the system, and check posted release schedules before driving to Minden.

## Hazards

Continuous current with few eddies at full release; the course is fenced and patrolled during events. Cold water outside midsummer.
