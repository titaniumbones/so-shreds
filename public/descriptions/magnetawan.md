## Details

The Lower Magnetawan near Britt is a Canadian Shield classic: granite pool-drop rapids in the II–III range spread along a wide, island-braided river between Highway 69 and Georgian Bay. Three Snye, Canal Rapids, and Thirty Dollar Rapids are the named sets most crews come for; big pools between drops make it a forgiving place to push your grade. Usually run as a weekend loop or an out-and-back from the Britt side.

Snowmelt off the Almaguin highlands keeps it juicy through May and June most years; it drops to a friendly technical level in midsummer.

## Levels

Bands here are scaled from the Britt gauge record (typical spring peak ≈ 200 m³/s, summer low ≈ 4) and will be refined as paddler reports come in — post one below if you've run it at a known level.

## Hazards

Big spring volume creates serious holes at Canal and Thirty Dollar; scout at anything over bank-full. Powerboat traffic near Britt on summer weekends.
